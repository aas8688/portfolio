About SCG

The club was founded in 1954, holding it's first meeting on the second floor of Galt City Hall. The old Dixon Dairy Stables in Galt served as the Clubs' indoor range. In 1957, the Club purchased the Grimer farm, a 100 acre site with a pond and a stream located on Gore Rd. Since then, another 150 acres have been added.

Archery

We hold 3 shoots per year (February, April and August) that are open to the general public. 40 assorted Full size 3D targets. 12 ring scoring. Please visit the Ontario Archery Association webpage for exact dates.

Clay Targets

Five Stand

Galt Sportsmens Club offers NSCA style Five-Stand with up to eight traps. A wide variety of target presentations will challenge shooters of all levels.

Open every:
Wednesday 4pm to 10pm
Sunday 9am to 5pm

FITASC

Galt Sportsmens Club offers up to two FITASC parcours. With five traps on each parcour featuring battues, midis, chandelles and rabbits. FITASC provides a challenging shooting experience.

Open every Sunday 9am to 5pm

Skeet

Galt Sportsmens Club offers five skeet fields with three available for regular practices, the aditioanl two are available for competitions and special events.

Open every:
Wednesday 4pm to 10pm
Sunday 9am to 5pm

Sporting Clay

Galt Sportsmens Club offers Ontario's largest sporting clays course with up to 17 stations every Sunday from 9am to 5pm. Set on over 50 acres of wetlands our course provides a realistic hunting environment that shooters of all levels can enjoy.


Open every:
Wednesday 4pm to 10pm and Sunday 9am to 5pm

Trap

Galt Sportsmens Club offers five trap fields featuring 16-yard, Handicap, wobble and doubles. Two fields are available for regular practice with an additional three available for competitions and special events.


Open every:
Wednesday 4pm to 10pm
Sunday 9am to 5pm

Weapon

Handgun

The Galt Sportsmens Club Handgun section has a 25 to 200 yard out door range that is approved for restricted handguns and rifles which is open to any legal hunting caliber to qualified members. (some restrictions apply) The outdoor Range is open 7 days a week (Mon to Fri 9am to sun-set. Wed until 10pm under lights. Sat & Sun 9am till 5pm. There is also an indoor range that is available 24/7. (Check for priority times)!!!

Rifle

Galt Sportsmens Club offers a 20-200 yard outdoor range and a 20-200 metre rim-fire silhouette range.

Indoor

Galt Sportsmens Club offers a 20-metre multi-discipline indoor range with member access 24/7. We have just completed a large renovation to the Air Handling system and the range is now heated in the winter months.
