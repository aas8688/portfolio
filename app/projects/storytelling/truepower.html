True Power is a sculpture located in UOIT’s Energy Systems and Nuclear Science Research Centre in the main lobby. It was unveiled on November 28, 2011 and was created by Ajax resident and artist Geordie Lishman. True Power represents the waves of energy and the power of man by the crafted wire along the horse.

True Power was a source of art to display the harnessing of energy and what better way to display such a remarkable piece than locating it inside a building that’s sole purpose is to find renewable energy sources.
